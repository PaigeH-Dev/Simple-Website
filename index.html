Guitars are versatile stringed instruments used in various music genres, from rock and blues to classical and jazz. They come in different types, including acoustic, electric, and bass guitars, each producing distinct sounds. Acoustic guitars rely on a hollow body to amplify sound, while electric guitars use pickups and amplifiers. Typically, guitars have six strings, though some variations have more. Played by strumming or picking, they allow for expressive melodies, complex chords, and dynamic solos, making them one of the most popular and widely played instruments worldwide.
